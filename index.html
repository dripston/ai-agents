Our Story

Tradition Since 1995

Founded by Rehaan Singh in a small roadside setup, our dhaba has grown into a beloved culinary landmark. For over 25 years, we've been serving authentic Indian cuisine cooked with traditional methods and fresh local ingredients.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic flavors of North India. Each dish is prepared with the same love and care that made our dhaba famous among travelers and food enthusiasts alike.

At Rehaan Ka Dhaba, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our warm hospitality and rustic ambiance create an unforgettable dining experience that keeps our customers coming back.
